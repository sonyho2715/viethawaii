## Next Steps: Add Your Envato Images

### To Replace Placeholders:

1. **Go to Envato Elements** (elements.envato.com)
2. **Download images** following `IMAGE-GUIDE.md`
3. **Replace files** in these folders:
   - `/public/images/hero/businesses-hero.jpg`
   - `/public/images/islands/` (6 images)
   - `/public/images/cta/community.jpg`
4. **Rebuild and deploy**:
   ```bash
   npm run build
   vercel --prod
   ```

### Recommended Envato Searches:

**Hero Image**:
- Search: "vietnamese pho overhead"
- Look for: Beautiful food photography, vibrant colors
- Size: 1920x1080px minimum

**Island Images**:
- Search: "hawaii beach" "tropical island" "palm trees"
- Look for: Beautiful scenery, inviting imagery
- Size: 800x800px (square)
- Can use same image 6 times or different ones

**CTA Image**:
- Search: "vietnamese community" "asian business owner"
- Look for: People, warm atmosphere, community feel
- Size: 1920x600px minimum
